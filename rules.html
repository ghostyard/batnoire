rules

both shelter and warning at once.

i’m k, my pronouns are she/they, i’m 25, my time zone’s pst, and there’s a lot to say here, so let’s get into it.

CONTENT WARNING this blog will have a lot of triggering content, including but not limited to: mental illness (c-ptsd, ocd, depresion, anxiety), violence, trauma, and current events. i tag for all of this as trigger tw, which translates as mental illness tw, drugs tw, etc. because of this, i will not write with minors.

ACTIVITY this is a low activity blog. i will be on and off here as my brain works, and if i’m not here, you might find me at @unerror.

SHIPPING i can ship, but i’m very careful about who i do and don’t ship with. bruce is naturally closed off as a person just as a self-protective measure, so patience is appreciated. i'm open to it! we just have to work with it.

WRITING & STYLE i don't use icons and i use normal sized text. i don't mind if other people use small text. double small text can be hard for me to read sometimes. i generally write in third person. i might get funky with it and do first person here and there as a treat.

ON CANON please note that i've recast bruce as a japanese american man. this means that canon changes, and the relationship with gotham also changes. i also divert from canon and follow ideas from batman: the imposter, wherein bruce was sent to military school at a young age and then disappeared to begin globetrotting once he turned eighteen. this creates an embittered, misanthropic guy.

INTERACTIONS down for whatever! ask memes, starter calls, all of it.
